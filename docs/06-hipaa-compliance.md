# HIPAA Compliance Guidance

This repository provides guidance for healthcare call tracking implementations. Ensure your organization complies with HIPAA requirements before storing or transmitting any Protected Health Information (PHI).

## Recommendations

- Avoid transmitting PHI through Google Ads or call tracking notes.
- Use call tags that describe outcomes without medical details.
- Ensure vendors sign Business Associate Agreements (BAA) when required.
- Limit user access and enable audit logs.
